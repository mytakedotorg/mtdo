MyTake is a radically transparent platform for citizens to speak their mind without political parties getting in the way. Here's how it works:

  • We start with a Foundation of unbiased Facts everyone can agree on. Only the following make the cut:
    • Presidential debates, 1980 - present. Always the full original source. No spin, no analysis, no cherry-picking, nothing out of context.
    • Our founding documents. The Declaration of Independence, the Articles of Confederation, and the United States Constitution.
  • Give your Take, and make it more convincing by adding snippets from this unbiased Foundation. You can't link to anything besides this Foundation.
  • Share your Take with friends, get their Take back.

Our only goal is to build a place where we can have civil disagreements based in reality. How can you trust us? You don't, just see for yourself.
